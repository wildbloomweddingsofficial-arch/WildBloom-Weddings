Who We Are

Crafting extraordinary
moments in the mountains

We are a boutique luxury event planning studio based in Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. Every celebration we create is a deeply personal expression of the couple or family we serve — rooted in the beauty of the Himalayas and elevated by our obsessive attention to detail.

Our team of 20+ specialists — from floral designers and lighting architects to venue coordinators and culinary curators — work in harmony to ensure your day is nothing short of magic.

Bespoke Planning No two events are alike. Tailored entirely to you.
Local Mastery Deep-rooted knowledge of Himachal's finest venues.
Floral Artistry Signature wildbloom designs by local artisans.
